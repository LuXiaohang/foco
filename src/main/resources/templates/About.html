About US

Who We Are

We are Hanyu and Xiaohang, two Cornell Tech students majoring in Information Science in Connective Media. We are building a platform to reduce food waste in hospitality sector by connecting food surplus to those in need based on food waste categories. Our goal is to apply technologies to alleviate food waste problems. We will release the beta version and rollout in Cornell Tech for test soon. Suggestions are welcomed.

Let's get connected. Drop us a note!

Why Food Waste

"Roughly one third of the food produced in the world for human consumption every year - approximately 1.3 million tonnes - get lost or wasted."

The level of food waste has been rising unprecedentedly, causing social, economic, and environmental impacts, and the fault is ours. We need to become aware of how much food we are wasting and start saving today.

What We Are Doing

In the past year, we have conducted extensive research to understand where does food waste come from in the food value chain and surveyed a range of existing food waste management solutions. Currently, food surplus is not collected nor used properly. The majority of food excesses end up in landfills while some of them can be repurposed. While mainstream views consider food waste is a result of low awareness, education, and regulation, we also recognize the inefficiencies in food planning, processing, and recycling. Moreover, we aim to reduce food waste by increasing food utilization. We are building a platform that connects food excesses to those in need based on different food use cases (e.g. food donations, composting, industrial use).
